Lasagna Recipe

Description of the lasagna dish

Layers of pasta with tomato sauce and ground beef, very tasty and easy to prepare

With our recipe, this dish could rapidly become one of your favorite ones!

Ingredients:

  • Fresh tomatoes
  • Ground beef
  • Lasagna pasta sheets
  • Gouda cheese

Steps:

  1. Prepare the tomato sauce in a pan
  2. Cook the ground beef
  3. Shred the cheese
  4. make layers of lasagna, tomato sauce, ground beef and shredded cheese
  5. put in the oven for 45 mins
